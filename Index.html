Vision and Mission

Vision

We strive to empower youth and mental health patients within our community and society at large, through skills, learning, training, and development for sustainability and to alleviate poverty.

Mission

  • To work towards empowerment and advancement of skills development and providing sustainable jobs.
  • Advancement of socio-economic and cultural welfare of the community.
  • To encourage humanity within families, community, and society.
  • To assist youth to get skills much needed for sustainable jobs opportunities.
  • To reduce illiteracy for unemployed youth within our society.
